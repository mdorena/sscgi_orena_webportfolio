About

My name is Mark Angelo D. Oreña. I am 23 years old and was born on May 24, 2001. I lived in San Blas, Villasis, Pangasinan. I recently graduated from Urdaneta City University with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. 

I am aspiring to become a software engineer. Despite being a fresh graduate, I gained valuable experience in a relevant college internship at iPhiTech IT and Digital Solutions, located in Mabalacat, Pampanga. Additionally, I gained hands-on experience in developing on both web and mobile platforms during our capstone project, "LendChecker: A web and mobile-based loan management system for Almonbevs Lending Corporation, Urdaneta City."

Knowledge of the following: Git, Github, Flutter, Dart, Firestore, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Wordpress (Elementor & Oxygen), Liquid, and Shopify.
